Everything Analytical – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 13 Aug 2025

1. Overview

This policy explains what we collect when you use Everything Analytical, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Data We Collect

  • From TikTok (with your consent): account identifiers (e.g., open_id), display name, avatar URL, and video metadata such as video id, title/description, duration, create_time, links, and public engagement counts (views/likes/comments/shares) where provided by TikTok’s APIs.
  • Technical data: basic logs (timestamps, IP, user agent) for security and troubleshooting.
  • Contact data: email address if you contact us.

3. How We Use Data

  • Provide analytics and recommendations about your content.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Service; prevent abuse; and support you.

4. Legal Bases

We process data based on your consent (TikTok OAuth) and our legitimate interests in providing and improving the Service.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may use service providers (e.g., cloud hosting) under agreements that protect your information. We may disclose information if required by law.

6. Retention

Access tokens and fetched video metadata are stored only as long as needed to provide analytics and are deleted within 30 days of last use. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures; however, no method is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region).

10. Revoking Access & Deletion

You can revoke our access in TikTok account settings at any time. For data deletion, email kemari.balfour@gmail.com.

11. Changes

We may update this policy and will post the new effective date above.

12. Contact
